Excel Tips & Tricks

Here are some cool tips and tricks to enhance your Excel skills:

  • Flash Fill: Automatically fill in data based on a pattern. Shortcut: Ctrl + E
  • Remove Duplicates: Quickly remove duplicate values from your data. Navigate to Data > Remove Duplicates.
  • Freeze Panes: Keep rows and columns visible while scrolling through your data. Navigate to View > Freeze Panes.
  • Conditional Formatting: Highlight cells based on their values. Navigate to Home > Conditional Formatting.
  • Pivot Tables: Summarize large datasets with Pivot Tables. Navigate to Insert > Pivot Table.
